About Us

We are a distinguished and professional legal partner, providing clients with all the necessary legal resources and solutions. Our law firm "Hedef Two" has been operating in the Kyrgyz market for more than 21 years, we provide legal support to citizens of China, Turkey, Iran, Pakistan, India, Afghanistan, Vietnam, Korea, Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Azerbaijan, Canada, Great Britain, the Netherlands, Germany , Poland, Lithuania, Latvia, Moldova, Russia, USA, and other countries.

We provide legal support from the moment of inviting foreign citizens to the territory of the Kyrgyz Republic and provide assistance in registering LLCs, ALCs, CJSCs, JSCs and joint ventures; in obtaining a work permit and a work book, family, tourist, exit, guest, study, business visas and a visa for visiting relatives. We have accountants on staff for bookkeeping, we provide assistance in registering at the place of residence, in obtaining a residence permit and citizenship of the Kyrgyz Republic.

Advocate
Kasymalieva Aizhan Urmakeevna

Kasymalieva Aizhan Urmakeevna is the General Director of the legal company "Hedef Dva" and also has been a Criminal and Civil Cases Attorney since 2003. She is the Chairperson of the Advocates' Board of the Oktyabrsky District of Bishkek. With 25 years of experience, she provides services in both judicial and pre-trial dispute resolution. Over the course of 20 years, the company has gained extensive expertise in providing legal services for company establishment and assisting clients in obtaining all necessary permits. We aim to deliver high-quality service tailored to the needs of our clients. Our profound understanding of legal intricacies allows us to develop individual strategies and solutions for each client.
